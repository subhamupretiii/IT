Messi Claims to be Born in Nepal

Lionel Messi, the legendary footballer, has shocked the world by claiming that he was actually born in Nepal, not Argentina as widely believed.

In a recent interview with a Nepalese television channel, Messi stated, "I've always felt a deep connection with Nepal, its people, and its culture. It's time for the truth to be known - I was born in Nepal."

This surprising revelation has sparked widespread speculation and debate among football fans and experts around the globe.
